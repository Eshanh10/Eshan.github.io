  • Thank you for your willingness to volunteer your time and skills. This project has the potential to make a real difference to those who are without a home and has the long-term goal of journeying with people towards a more stable living situation.     Coronavirus (COVID-19)

    We endeavour to ensure the safety of all volunteers, staff and guests and are following recommendations of both the Federal Government and the Victoria State Government. Please answer the following questions to ascertain your suitability to proceed with volunteering.

  • Please read the following statements carefully:

    • I have not been in contact with anyone infected with COVID-19 in the last 14 days or travelled from overseas in the last 14 days
    • I am not unwell or displaying symptoms of coronavirus (COVID-19) – fever, breathing difficulties, breathlessness, cough, sore throat, fatigue or tiredness
    • I will undertake the specific health and safety training required in relation to COVID-19
    • I will alert DSK SCHOOL immediately if my situation changes and I am no longer eligible under COVID-19 regulations to volunteer.

  • We appreciate your desire to volunteer. At this time, however, we are advised that it is unsafe to accept your application. We will add your name to our mailing list so that you can stay up to date with our activities and look for other ways to get involved. If we still have a need for volunteers when restrictions are amended, we will make our needs known.
